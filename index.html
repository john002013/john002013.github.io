About Me

Robotics Researcher & Engineering Student

I am a final year student from the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, currently standing as the 2nd best graduating student of the class of 2026. My research interests lie in precision agriculture, human-robot interaction, autonomous robots, and robot perception.

Having developed a passion for robotics from the age of 8, where I built robots using scraps, I immersed myself in ROS, Embedded Systems, Computer Vision, PyTorch-based deep learning, and technical research documentation.

My long-term goal is to become a professor of robotics and give back through teaching, mentorship, and impactful research.
