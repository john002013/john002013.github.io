About Me

I am a final-year student of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, currently ranked as the second-best graduating student of my cohort. My passion for robotics began at the age of eight, building simple robots from scraps, and later matured into a focused academic and research pursuit after laboratory exposure.

I have worked extensively across robotics software and hardware stacks, including ROS 2, embedded systems, computer vision, deep learning, and agent-based AI. My long-term goal is to become a professor of robotics and contribute to society through education, research, and accessible innovation.
