About Me

JB

Robotics Researcher & Engineering Student

I am a final year student from the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, currently standing as the 2nd best graduating student of the class of 2026. My research interests lie in precision agriculture, human-robot interaction, autonomous robots, and robot perception.

During the course of my undergraduate journey, and having developed a passion for robotics starting from the age of 8 where I built robots using scraps, I immersed myself in many self-paced learning curves. I learned ROS (Robot Operating System), Embedded Systems, Computer Vision, PyTorch advanced deep learning techniques, technical report documentation, and more.

My long-term goal is to become a professor of robotics, holding the belief that the best way to impact the world in a positive way is to teach robotics. I am committed to exposing, teaching, and giving back to my society in terms of STEM education, innovation, and research.
